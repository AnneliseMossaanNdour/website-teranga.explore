Who are we ?

Founded in March 2023 by Annelise Ndour, a travel, tourism and hotel management enthusiast. Our travel agency specializes in tourist tours in Senegal. Her goal, to dazzle you and make you spend the best time in this country that has so much to offer.

Why choose Senegal ?

Senegal is an increasingly popular tourist destination, and it's not hard to see why. Between its rich culture, delicious cuisine and spectacular scenery, there's something for everyone. Whether you're looking to relax on white sandy beaches, explore historic cities or go on a wildlife safari, Senegal has it all. You can also experience traditional music and dance, as well as colorful markets where you can purchase unique souvenirs. All in all, Senegal is a destination well worth visiting for an authentic cultural experience and unforgettable moments.
